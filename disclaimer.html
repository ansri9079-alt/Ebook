Why no guarantee?

  • · Scam tactics evolve in real-time and may outpace the guidance in this edition of the Handbook.
  • · Individual circumstances, app versions, device settings, and network conditions vary widely.
  • · Outcomes depend entirely on the reader's understanding and application of the guidance provided.
  • · Determined and sophisticated bad actors may bypass even the most diligent safety measures.

The Handbook is a tool to help you make more informed decisions — not a security product or technical safeguard. Think of it as an educational supplement to, not a replacement for, vigilance and professional security tools.

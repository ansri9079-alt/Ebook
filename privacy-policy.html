Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how UPISecure collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or purchase our digital products. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01 — Overview

Overview

UPISecure ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website at upisecure.com and sells the UPI Safety Handbook as a digital download. This Privacy Policy governs all data we collect from visitors, purchasers, and subscribers. It applies wherever you access our content — web browser, mobile device, or email.

We are committed to handling your data responsibly. We collect only what is necessary, never sell your personal information, and give you control over your data at all times.

02 — Collection

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you provide directly, and information collected automatically when you use our site.

Information You Provide

  • ›Name and email address (during purchase or newsletter signup)
  • ›Billing information processed securely by our payment provider
  • ›Messages sent to our support email
  • ›Voluntary feedback or testimonials submitted

Collected Automatically

  • ›Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • ›Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • ›Referring URLs and exit pages
  • ›IP address (anonymized for analytics)
03 — Usage

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect only for legitimate, transparent purposes:

1

Fulfilling Your Purchase

To send you the ebook download link and any future free updates to the same edition.

2

Customer Support

To respond to your queries, resolve issues, and provide assistance related to your purchase.

3

Improving Our Products

Anonymized analytics help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve the experience and content.

4

Marketing Communications (Opt-in Only)

Only if you explicitly subscribe, we may send you security tips, new product announcements, or promotional offers. You can unsubscribe at any time.

04 — Sharing

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties. Ever.

We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • · Payment Processors: Your billing data is handled by our secure payment gateway (e.g., Razorpay or equivalent). We do not store credit card or UPI credentials on our servers.
  • · Email Service Providers: To deliver your ebook and transactional emails, we use a trusted email delivery service (e.g., Mailchimp or SendGrid). These providers are bound by strict data-processing agreements.
  • · Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of UPISecure or others, we may disclose information to law enforcement or regulatory authorities.
05 — Cookies

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies to enhance your experience. Here is a breakdown of the cookies we use:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
color-theme Stores your dark/light mode preference Persistent (localStorage)
dismissedExitIntent Remembers if you dismissed the discount popup Session (localStorage)
_analytics Anonymized page view analytics (no personal data) 30 days

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the website, such as theme preference persistence.

06 — Security

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

SSL Encryption

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols.

Secure Payments

Card data is handled by PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways.

Minimal Data Storage

We only store what is necessary and purge data on request.

While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% immune to breaches. In the unlikely event of a data incident affecting your information, we will notify you promptly and take immediate corrective action.

07 — Your Rights

Your Rights

You have the following rights over your personal data. To exercise any of them, contact us at support@upisecure.com:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Right to Correction

Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information about you.

Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.

Right to Opt-Out of Marketing

Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in any email we send.

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format upon request.

08 — Retention

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy or as required by applicable law:

  • · Purchase data (name, email, order records) is retained for up to 7 years for accounting and legal compliance.
  • · Support correspondence is retained for up to 2 years after resolution.
  • · Analytics data is anonymized and retained for up to 24 months.
  • · Marketing email lists are purged within 30 days of an unsubscribe request.
09 — Links

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external resources such as the National Cybercrime Reporting Portal or government advisories. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we have no control over or responsibility for their content or data practices. We encourage you to review their policies before sharing any information.

10 — Children

Children's Privacy

UPISecure is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@upisecure.com and we will delete the information promptly.

11 — Updates

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you by email.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of our website after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
